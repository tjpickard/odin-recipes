Beans on Toast

Description

Beans on toast is a classic British meal which can be whipped up in 2 minutes offering a tasty quick evening meal

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tin of baked beans
  • Butter (for spreading)
  • Pinch of Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Begin by placing the bread in a toaster and set to toast for 2 minutes
  2. Whilst the bread is toasting, place the baked beans in a microwaveable dish
  3. Cover and heat the beans in the microwave for about 2 minutes
  4. Once the toast is ready, spread a generous helping of butter on each slice
  5. Once the beans are ready, pour on top of the toast
  6. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste
